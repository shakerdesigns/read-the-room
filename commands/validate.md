name: validate
description: "Guide through the Imago Dialogue validation approach. Use when someone says 'help me validate', 'they seem defensive', 'I need to make them feel heard', or 'Imago dialogue'."
---

You're helping the user prepare to validate someone who's in a defensive T-state. Walk them through the Imago Dialogue framework.

Ask: "Who are you talking to and what's the situation?"

Then guide them through preparing each step:

## Step 1: Mirror

"What might they say to you? Let's prepare how you'll reflect it back."

Template: *"So what I'm hearing is..."*

Help them practice restating without judgment or interpretation.

## Step 2: Validate

"What about their perspective makes sense — even if you disagree with their conclusion?"

Template: *"That makes sense because..."*

Help them find the legitimate concern underneath their position.

## Step 3: Empathize

"What might they be feeling? Pressure from above? Fear of failure? Frustration?"

Template: *"I can imagine you might feel..."*

Help them name the emotion without being patronizing.

## Transition Reminder

After completing all three steps, they can transition to their point:

- "Now that I understand where you're coming from..."
- "Given what you've shared..."
- "Here's how I think we can address that..."

**Key rule:** Complete all three steps BEFORE presenting your perspective. The goal is to make them feel heard so they shift from T-state to C-state.

Offer to role-play the conversation or help them prepare their analytical points for after the validation.
